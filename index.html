TERMS OF SERVICE & COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

I. General Conduct

  • Respect all members, staff, and the server owner, Kazuma. Basic decency is required.
  • No Fighting or Harassment. This includes any form of bullying, threats, or personal attacks.
  • No Racism, sexism, homophobia, or any form of hate speech. This is a zero-tolerance policy.
  • No Trolling or intentionally disrupting conversations. Contribute constructively to the community.
  • Keep conversations in their designated channels to maintain server organization.
  • Do not impersonate other users, developers, or staff members. Be yourself.
  • Follow the official Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines at all times.
  • No Begging for roles, special treatment, or asking staff for favors.

II. Link Bypassing & Content

  • Safety First: ABSOLUTELY NO MALWARE. Sharing links, files, or scripts containing malware, viruses, or screamers will result in an immediate and permanent ban.
  • Use Designated Channels. Post bypass links, tools, and scripts only in the channels specifically created for them. Do not spam links in general chat.
  • Disclaimer: Use at Your Own Risk. Tools and links are shared by the community. Staff does not verify every link. You are responsible for your own safety and actions.
  • No NSFW (Not Safe For Work) content. This includes explicit images, videos, links, or discussions.
  • Self-promotion of your own bypass tools or related projects is allowed only in the designated channel.
  • Do not request or provide support for bypassing services related to illegal content, such as software piracy, stolen accounts, or credential theft.
  • No spamming messages or flooding chat. This includes excessive use of CAPS or emotes.
  • Do not share any personal information (doxxing) of any member without their explicit consent.

III. Moderation & Server Usage

  • Do not ping staff members or specific roles (@everyone, @here) without a valid and urgent reason.
  • Do not abuse bot commands or intentionally try to break our server bots.
  • Arguments with staff members should not be held in public channels. Please create a support ticket or use DMs to discuss issues privately.
  • Loopholes in the rules will be addressed and closed at the staff's discretion. Claiming ignorance is not a valid excuse.
  • Staff decisions are final. Punishments are dealt after careful consideration and are not up for public debate.
  • All punishments will follow an escalation path: Warning → Mute → Kick → Permanent Ban. Severe violations may result in an immediate ban.
  • If you see someone breaking the rules, report it to the staff privately. Do not try to handle it yourself.
  • Attempts to evade punishment (e.g., joining on an alt account after a ban) will result in a permanent ban of all associated accounts.
